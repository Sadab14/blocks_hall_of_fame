BLOCKS Hall of Fame

BLOCKS Hall of Fame celebrates collectors who share their passion for Minifigures. Points, milestones, and ranks are updated Monthly, highlighting top fans across Bangladesh.

Rules

  1. Each registered collector will get a "Collector_tag" from us and their database will be created, in which their total number of collected minifigures and points will be updated.
  2. After purchasing a minifigure from BLOCKS it can be registered in the collectors database and points will be added automatically
  3. You will get points for each minifigure you purchase and register in your database.
  4. Only minifigures bought from BLOCKS can be registered in your database.
  5. Traded minifigures can be registered if only the minifigures was not previously registered in any collectors database and each traded minifigure will carry 1 point.
  6. To register, you have to click a pic of your minifigure and post it in the Lego Minifigure Collectors BD LMCB group with your collector tag and following tags #blockssnapthat #blocks #lcmb #blockshalloffame.
  7. For the bulk register, max 5 minifigures can be included in one frame.
  8. Minifigures can be registered within 30 days of purchase.
  9. Those who purchased minifigures from us before can register their old minifigures from BLOCKS in their database within 30 days from the day of registration in Hall of Fame
  10. Minifigures from build sets cannot be registered. Only solo-purchased minifigures can be registered.
  11. Existing customers can enter with their headstart, but in that case they cannot claim previous milestone gifts; only the last milestone gift can be claimed. Or you can start from scratch.
  12. Points and positions will be updated monthly in the Hall of Fame.
  13. Reward Minifigures will not carry points.
  14. Any fraudulent registrations will lead to disqualification.
  15. BLOCKS reserves the right to amend the rules at any time without prior notice.
  16. By registering, you agree to allow BLOCKS to use your submitted photos for promotional purposes.
  17. Decisions made by BLOCKS regarding the Hall of Fame are final and binding.
