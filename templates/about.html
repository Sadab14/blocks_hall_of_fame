BLOCKS Hall of Fame

BLOCKS Hall of Fame celebrates collectors who share their passion for Minifigures. Points, milestones, and ranks are updated weekly, highlighting top fans across the globe.

Rules

  1. You will get points for each minifig you purchase and register in your database.
  2. Only minifigs bought from BLOCKS can be registered in your database.
  3. To register, you have to click a pic of your minifig and post it in the Lego Minifigure Collectors BD LMCB group with tags #blockssnapthat #blocks #lcmb #blockshalloffame.
  4. For the bulk register, max 10 minifigs can be included in one frame.
  5. Minifigures from build sets cannot be registered. Only solo-purchased minifigs can be registered.
  6. Existing customers can enter with their headstart, but in that case they cannot claim previous milestone gifts; only the last milestone gift can be claimed. Or you can start from scratch.
  7. Points and positions will be updated weekly in the Hall of Fame.
  8. Top 3 places will get access to exclusive products and pre-orders, and receive gifts and discounts.
  9. Minifigs can be registered within 30 days of purchase.
  10. Reward Minifigures will not carry points.
  11. Any fraudulent registrations will lead to disqualification.
  12. BLOCKS reserves the right to amend the rules at any time without prior notice.
  13. By registering, you agree to allow BLOCKS to use your submitted photos for promotional purposes.
  14. Decisions made by BLOCKS regarding the Hall of Fame are final and binding.
